Sour Cream Enchiladas

Description

Chicken enchiladas with a sour cream sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh
  • Sour cream
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Chicken Broth
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to make

  1. Saute onion and garlic
  2. Brown chicken
  3. Shred chicken
  4. Make sauce
  5. Put chicken in tortillas
  6. Put in casserole dish
  7. Put sauce on top
  8. Put cheese on top
  9. Put in oven
  10. Serve
