Nashville Hot Chicken

Description

Fried chicken drenched in a home made hot sauce served on white bread

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • breading
  • Hot Sauce
  • White Bread

Steps to make

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk
  2. Bread chicken
  3. Fry chicken
  4. Make hot sauce
  5. Put hot sauce on chicken
  6. Serve on white bread
