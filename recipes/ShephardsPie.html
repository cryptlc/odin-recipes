Shephards Pie

Description

Beef and vegetables with a potatoe topping

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Minced Beef
  • Mixed Veggies
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Beef Broth
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Flour

Steps to make

  1. Make mashed potatoes
  2. Chop onion and garlic
  3. Saute onion and garlic
  4. Brown beef
  5. Add flour
  6. Add broth
  7. Add tomatoe paste
  8. Add mixed veggies
  9. Put in casserole dish
  10. Put mashed potatoes on top
  11. Put in oven
  12. Serve
